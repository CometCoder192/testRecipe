Nestle's Maggi, the instant noodle

Recipe- All you need is 4 cups of water, 2 packets of the noodles, and the tastemaker which is included. Voila, mix them up and much it while it's hot.


    Ingredients


  • Maggi Noodles, 2 packs
  • Water, 4 cups
  • Chopped Veggies (Optional)

    Steps


  1. Boil the water along with the noodles
  2. Add the tastemaker roughly one minute while the water boils
  3. Stir well to ensure to ensure the best taste, and serve hot.
